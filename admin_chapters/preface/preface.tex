\chapter{Preface}

The ExploTest tool was originally conceived by Zihao Huang under the supervision of Dr. Caroline Lemieux. The implementation described in this thesis is a substantial rewrite by Kevin Liu and me, and shares no code with Huang's original.

I identified the research questions, designed the evaluation methodology, and performed all analysis of the research data presented in this thesis, with advice from Kevin Liu, Zahin Wahab, and Dr. Caroline Lemieux.
